  • Header Row is mandatory
  • Transaction Type : Income / Expense , Label , Initial Currency : INR / JPY / ... , Initial Amount , Category , Transaction Mode : UPI / Credit Card / ... , Date : DD-MM-YYYY. Ex : 01-07-2026
